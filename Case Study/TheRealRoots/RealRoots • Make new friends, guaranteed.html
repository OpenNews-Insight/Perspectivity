HOW IT WORKS

Make real connections, build lasting friendships.

Tell us about yourself

Fill out a short survey and get matched with a group of like-minded women in your city.

Meet your group in person

An amazing RealRoots guide will lead your group through guided conversations over a glass of wine or non-alcoholic bev.

Enjoy 6 weeks of curated experiences

If you love your gal group, you'll continue with a 6 week series. We reserve, match, and organize you just arrive and enjoy.

Join the larger RealRoots grad community

After your 6 week series, join the larger RealRoots grad community, including weekly events, 1-1 matches, and a private group chat.

The easy way to make friends

No swiping,
no small talk.

Every RealRoots experience is led by an amazing guide who facilitates deep conversation and fun activities with your group. It's the fast-track way to make a new group of friends, without any of the awkwardness.
