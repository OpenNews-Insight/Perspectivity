Just some fun stuff

In preference trials, Pakistani listeners consistently preferred Orator’s voice over competing Urdu-speaking models — including those from Microsoft and OpenAI.

As of February 2025, OpenAI’s speech models, including their largest speech models and real-time API, demonstrated hallucinations in over 20% of instances during an evaluation of 10,000 generations.

Microsoft TTS struggled with scripts containing English words. It often completely skipped English words. English words in Urdu scripts is a common occurrence in medical, agricultural, educational, and news contexts.
