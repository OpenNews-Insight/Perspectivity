About Us

We are a foundational Voice AI company focused on Pakistani languages.

We develop Speech Understanding and Speech Generation models that outperform all competition in Pakistani languages. We make these models available through reliable and affordable APIs, SDKs, and Web Interfaces; enabling businesses to offer digital services through an intuitive, voice-first experience that just works.

Our team has a decade of experience building world-leading voice products, including at Alexa and Siri. Software written by us is used by millions daily.

We do in-house research and development. We train and deploy our own models. We own our technology stack end-to-end.
